Showcase: Tea Botanical.

The project required the creation of a unique drink-based brand, its packaging, and supporting advertisements. This incorporates the skills of branding, product design, and marketing materials designed using Adobe Suite software skills learned throughout the program.

The design of the product packaging began soon after the branding elements were cemented. The brand imagery and style elements were simple but also popped effectively which allowed the product design to still retain the same consistent visual appearance while having enough appropriate space to present mandatory content such as nutritional information, product flavor and more.The type of packaging was also decided upon based on the general tone of the brand. Simple shape based boxes and cannisters with minimal colors to let the brand itself pop and effectively speak for itself in a confident and subdued manner

The design direction of the advertisements geared towards a chill, comfortable tone, with colors representing the natural and plant-based aspects, while still having a unique aspect of modern minimal design with good amounts of negative space to let the various aspects breath and have a good consistency. Roughs were also created for this aspect and were also reviewed and polished for the result. Additionally, stock images that felt appropriate for the brand and design intention were found and used. A challenge of this component was the balance of creating advertisements that were eye-catching and appealing, while still being consistent with the brand styles defined. Through feedback sessions, a good balance of imagery, brand assets, and negative space was decided upon for the result.

A style guide was created prior to creation of the packaging and advertisting. This was done to reinforce a consistent presentation across the product components to keep them in line with a brand in mind. Also critical, is the benefit to collaboration as it allows for multiple people to easily maintain brand standards.

Made using Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator.

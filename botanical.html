Showcase: Tea Botanical.

An organic tea brand showcasing: product design advertisement design, and design systems.

The design of the product packaging uses a minimalistic style and text to let the brand pop with its color and imagery.

The advertisement designs are geared towards a relaxed and comfortable tone, with colors representing the natural aspects of the brand.

A style guide with various standards was set up to allow for brand consistency across deliverables.

Made using Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator.
